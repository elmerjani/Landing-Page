Animes you should watch

Death Note

Death Note is a psychological thriller anime that follows Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook called the "Death Note." This notebook allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it, as long as he knows their face. Driven by a desire to rid the world of criminals, Light adopts the persona of "Kira" and begins his quest for justice. However, his actions attract the attention of a brilliant detective known as L, leading to a tense battle of wits and morality between the two.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The remnants of mankind live within massive walled cities to protect themselves. The story follows Eren Yeager, his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert, who join the military's Scout Regiment to fight the Titans after their hometown is destroyed. As they battle the Titans, they uncover shocking truths about the origin of the Titans, humanity's history, and the true nature of their world.

Kuroko no Basket

Kuroko no Basket is a sports anime that revolves around high school basketball. The story follows Tetsuya Kuroko, a seemingly unremarkable player who was once part of the legendary "Generation of Miracles" team in middle school. Now in high school, Kuroko joins Seirin High's basketball team alongside the talented Taiga Kagami, who has just returned from the U.S. Despite his lack of physical presence and scoring ability, Kuroko excels at misdirection and passing, making him an invaluable player. Together, Kuroko and Kagami aim to defeat Kuroko's former "Generation of Miracles" teammates and become the best in Japan.
